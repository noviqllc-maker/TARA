from your exact birth date, time, and place — with modern wellness signals to help you
understand your daily energy, emotions, timing, and direction.

planetary positions, and Vimśottarī dasha periods are computed astronomically for you, not
pulled from a one-size-fits-all sun sign.

WHAT TARA OFFERS
• Your real Vedic birth chart — planets, houses, nakshatra, and dashas
• Daily cosmic weather — today's moon phase, panchanga, and your personal transits
• Tara AI — a 24/7 guide that knows your chart and answers your questions
• Life Timeline — your major planetary life periods, past to future
• Daily insights — emotional, mental, relationship, career, body, and spiritual guidance
• Mood journal — track how you feel and reflect over time
• Wellness integration — connect Apple Health and wearables to blend body and chart

Tara provides astrology and wellness insights for reflection and lifestyle support.
