# Tara — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: [DATE]_

Tara ("we", "our", "the app") provides Vedic astrology and wellness insights for
reflection and lifestyle support. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.
**Tara does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.**

## Information we collect

**Birth details you provide.** Your name, birth date, birth time, and birth place. These
are used solely to calculate your astrological birth chart. They are stored locally on your
device and, if you create an account, on our secure servers.

**Wellness data (optional).** If you connect a wellness source (Apple Health, Google Health
Connect, Oura, WHOOP, Fitbit, or Garmin), Tara reads metrics such as sleep, recovery, heart
rate, HRV, steps, and activity to generate lifestyle insights. This data is used only to
produce your insights and is never sold.

**Journal entries.** Mood, energy, stress, and notes you log are stored to show your history
and personalize guidance.

**AI conversations.** Questions you ask Tara AI are processed to generate responses. They may
be sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) through our secure server to produce answers.

## How we use your information
- To calculate your birth chart and daily insights.
- To personalize guidance using your chart and (if connected) wellness data.
- To operate the Tara AI assistant.
- To manage subscriptions and app functionality.

## What we do NOT do
- We do not sell your personal data.
- We do not use your health data for advertising.
- We do not share your birth details or wellness data with third parties except the
  service providers strictly necessary to run the app (e.g. our hosting and AI provider).

## Data storage and security
Your data is stored on your device and on secure managed infrastructure. We use industry-
standard measures to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Your choices
- You can disconnect wellness sources at any time in Settings.
- You can delete your data by resetting the app or contacting us.
- You can cancel a subscription through your app store account.

## Children
Tara is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region). We do not
knowingly collect data from children.

## Third-party services
Tara uses: Anthropic (AI responses), Google Places (birthplace lookup), RevenueCat / Apple /
Google (subscriptions), and your chosen wellness providers. Each has its own privacy policy.

## Changes
We may update this policy; material changes will be noted in the app.
